F1 Movie Details

  • Directed by: Joseph Kosinski (Top Gun: Maverick)
  • Produced by: Jerry Bruckheimer & Lewis Hamilton (F1 Champion)
  • Starring Brad Pitt , Damson Idris ,Kerry Condon ,Javier Bardem,Sarah Niles

About Movie

The movie tells the story of Sonny Hayes (played by Brad Pitt), a former Formula 1 driver who once had a promising career but left the sport after a serious crash and internal team conflicts. Years later, he is invited back to mentor a rising young driver, Joshua Pearce (played by Damson Idris), on a fictional F1 team called APX GP.Sonny returns not only as a mentor but also gets a second chance to race alongside the current generation of elite F1 drivers. The film explores themes of redemption, legacy, and the intense mental and physical pressure of racing at the highest level.

Key Highlights of F1 (2025)

Realism Like Never Before :

  • The film was shot during live F1 Grand Prix events (like Silverstone and Monza)
  • Real F1 teams and drivers were on the same track as Brad Pitt’s character.
  • No green screen used for races — all footage is practical and real-tim

Fictional Team: APX GP :

  • The movie introduces an 11th F1 team, not part of the real 2025 season.
  • APX GP has its own logo, uniforms, garage, pit wall, and race cars.
  • Their car was developed in collaboration with Mercedes-AMG and Carlin Motorsport.

IMAX & Cinematic Tech :

  • Custom-built camera rigs were mounted inside the APX GP cars.
  • Shot in high-resolution IMAX, providing an immersive racing experience.
  • Director Joseph Kosinski focused on "being in the driver’s seat" realism, like in Top Gun: Maverick.
