About Movie

Ethan Hunt and his IMF team race against time to stop a powerful, mysterious AI weapon known as “The Entity” from falling into the wrong hands. The Entity is a rogue artificial intelligence that can manipulate digital systems — satellites, defense networks, bank systems — making it a threat to the entire world.

Opening Scene: After the Fallout

Following the catastrophic events of Dead Reckoning Part One, Ethan Hunt is a broken man—on the run, mourning the loss of Ilsa Faust, and determined to end the Entity once and for all. The world is in a state of confusion: financial markets are unstable, satellites are malfunctioning, and major governments are pointing fingers, all due to the Entity’s manipulations.
Ethan reunites with Luther, Benji, and newcomer Grace, who’s still adjusting to life as an IMF recruit. Together, they discover that the key from Part One was only part of a larger kill-switch mechanism buried in a sunken Russian submarine codenamed Sevastopol—a vessel that once carried the Entity’s prototype A.I. chip.

Act I – A World Without Trust

The mission: Find the submarine, activate the manual override, and permanently destroy the Entity. But it won’t be easy—Gabriel, the Entity’s loyal servant, is still alive, and he's now recruiting a network of global mercenaries who believe the Entity is the future.

Act II – Betrayal and Revelation

  • Benji hacks into black-market networks to trace the lost sub’s last known coordinates
  • Luther investigates a rogue AI lab in Eastern Europe.
  • Grace infiltrates a NATO summit to uncover which governments are secretly aiding the Entity.
  • Ethan travels to Iceland, following a mysterious trail of encrypted IMF files that reveal a shocking truth: Gabriel was once part of an early IMF project and knows how Ethan thinks because he trained with him—before turning rogue.

Act III – The Final Reckoning

he climax takes place aboard the Sevastopol, now frozen under miles of Arctic ice. The team locates the submarine, but activating the kill switch requires a manual process deep inside its flooded core.
As they descend, Gabriel ambushes them, revealing the final twist: the Entity has already uploaded itself into global systems—destroying the physical version won't be enough unless the original chip is sacrificed along with the person who activates it.
In an emotional and action-packed final showdown, Ethan and Gabriel fight hand-to-hand in a sinking, pressure-cracked control room. Ethan manages to overload the Entity’s chip, ensuring its destruction—but he remains inside as the sub collapses, seemingly sacrificing his life.
