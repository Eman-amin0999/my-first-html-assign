About Movie

Set five years after the events of Jurassic World: Dominion, the Earth has changed. Due to rapid climate shifts, habitat loss, and disease outbreaks, most dinosaur species have either died out or been driven into a few isolated equatorial regions, where they continue to evolve in secret.
A powerful pharmaceutical corporation, desperate to find a breakthrough treatment for a growing global heart disease crisis, believes the answer may lie in the unique biology of three massive dinosaurs:
  • The Titanosaurus (land)
  • The Mosasaurus (sea)
  • The Quetzalcoatlus (air)

To retrieve samples from these ancient giants, the company assembles a covert expedition led by Zora Bennett (Scarlett Johansson), a former military specialist with a history of success in extreme rescue missions. Alongside her are mercenaries, biologists, and survival experts — but none of them are truly prepared for what awaits.

Rising Conflict

As the team moves deeper into the island, they uncover:

  • A hidden underground lab containing unfinished genetic experiments
  • Signs that some dinosaurs have hybrid trait
  • A rival corporate team, secretly sent by the pharma company to ensure the mission succeeds — at any cost
